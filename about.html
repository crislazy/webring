Joining

To join, you need to first follow these rules:

  • Your website does not contain any NSFW, racist or hateful content.
  • You must add the webring widget to your site.

If you follow these rules, submit a PR to this repo, adding a new json entry to the sites.json file(replacing "<name> and "<url> with your website's name and url) or emailing me at me [at] crislzy [dot] xyz:

{"name": "<name>","url": "<url>"}
